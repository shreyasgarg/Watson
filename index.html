Welcome to Watson News
This is a webapp that uses Natural Language Processing (courtesy of the IBM Waton Data Alchemy API) to search all possible news articles with added capability of grouping of ideas, relationship between key words, as well as sentiment analysis
Taxonomy The overarching idea behind the search
Concept An idea within the taxonomy
Key Words Key word you are looking for
Sentiment A number between 0 and 1, with .5 being neither positive nor negative
